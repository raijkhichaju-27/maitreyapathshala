Volunteer in our School and Biodynamic Farm

Join us as a volunteer at our Waldorf School and take part in a variety of hands-on, meaningful activities. Whether you're helping in the classroom or contributing to the daily rhythm of the school and farm, your support makes a real difference.

As a volunteer, you can engage in:

  • Various activities of school such as handwork, woodwork, gardening, painting, music, games etc.
  • Assisting the class teacher in daily lessons and classroom activities
  • Cleaning and maintaining the school — caring for our beautiful learning spaces
  • Farm activities — planting, harvesting, composting and nurturing the land on our biodynamic farm

Our Biodynamic Farm

Our school is deeply connected to an 8-acre biodynamic farm. Volunteers have the unique opportunity to work the land using sustainable, anthroposophical farming practices — experiencing the living connection between earth, food, and community that is at the heart of Waldorf education.

If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us. We look forward to welcoming you into our community!
