About Maitreya Pathshala

Discover our journey, mission, and commitment to holistic Waldorf education in Pokhara

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach and school community

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing Waldorf educational environment that fosters holistic development, creativity, and academic excellence. We aim to cultivate curious, compassionate, and confident individuals who contribute positively to society while honoring their unique potential.

Our Vision

To be Nepal's leading Waldorf educational institution, recognized for developing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, think critically, act compassionately, and live purposefully. We envision a community where every child discovers their passion and potential.

Our Journey

A timeline of our school's development and milestones

2010

Foundation & Inception

Maitreya Pathshala was founded by a group of educators passionate about bringing Waldorf education to Pokhara. Started with just 15 students and 3 teachers in a small rented building.

2013

First Permanent Campus

Acquired our current campus location and began construction of purpose-built Waldorf classrooms, art studios, and outdoor learning spaces designed for holistic education.

2016

Curriculum Expansion

Expanded our program to include Grades 4-6 and introduced specialized Waldorf teacher training programs for our faculty, enhancing educational quality and consistency.

2019

Community Recognition

Received recognition from the Nepal Waldorf Association for excellence in implementing Waldorf pedagogy. Student enrollment grew to over 200 students across all grades.

2022

Modern Facilities & Technology

Completed construction of new science labs, music rooms, and computer facilities while maintaining Waldorf's balance between technology and hands-on learning.

2024

Current Status & Future

Now serving 300+ students with 35+ dedicated faculty members. Planning expansion of high school program and international exchange partnerships with Waldorf schools worldwide.
