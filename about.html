About Maitreya Pathshala

Discover our journey, mission, and commitment to holistic Waldorf education in Pokhara

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach and school community

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing Waldorf educational environment that fosters holistic development, creativity, and academic excellence. We aim to cultivate curious, compassionate, and confident individuals who contribute positively to society while honoring their unique potential.

Our Vision

To be Nepal's leading Waldorf educational institution, recognized for developing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, think critically, act compassionately, and live purposefully. We envision a community where every child discovers their passion and potential.

Our Journey

A timeline of our school's development and milestones

2011

Foundation & Inception

The seed of anthroposophy sprouted in Pokhara with the biodynamic farm on 8 acres of land, driven by the great heart of Babita Tulachan Kayastha. Supported by Ritman Gurung and Bishnu Kumari Sherchan, the farm supplied its products to the community and created a small anthroposophical community with programs, seminars, workshops, and study.

2014

Waldorf Kindergarten Opens

The anthroposophical community organically requested for the Waldorf Kindergarten. The door of Waldorf education opened with 15 children and 3 teachers in Pokhara. Since then, we have upgraded one grade every year.

2015

Starting Grade 1

We started our grade 1 with Ritman Gurung as a class teacher.

2022

First Cycle Completed

Ritman Gurung finished his first cycle of class teaching.

2023

High School Upgrade

We upgraded our school with high school for classes 9 and 10. Our school now offers education from kindergarten to grade 10.

2025

First SEE Board Examination

Three students of our school attended the SEE board examination of Nepal, and all three of them passed the examination successfully.
