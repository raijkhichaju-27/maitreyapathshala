# Website Content Integration Log

This document outlines how the comprehensive curriculum details from the "Journey" document were integrated into the Maitreya Pathshala website. 

Because we were restricted from adding new HTML pages, the curriculum information was woven directly into existing pages to enrich the site content without overwhelming the layout.

## 1. Updates to the Home Page (`index.html`)

The homepage's **"Classes Available"** section was updated to reflect the premium, Waldorf-aligned language from the provided document.

**Changes Made:**
*   **Subtitle:** Updated from "Kindergarten to Grade 9" to **"Kindergarten to Grade 10"** to accurately reflect the school's current high school offerings.
*   **Waldorf Kindergarten Card:** Updated the description to emphasize the environment: *"Ages 3-6 | A sanctuary of wonder, warmth, and unhurried childhood rooted in rhythms of nature and magic of play."*
*   **Grade 1-8 Card:** Updated the description to reflect the Waldorf pedagogy: *"A living education through meaning, artistic richness, and developmentally attuned main lesson blocks."*
*   **Class 9-10 Card:** Updated the title (from Grade 9-10) and description to highlight the focus on the upper school: *"Age 14-16 | A rigorous and holistic curriculum emphasizing integration, depth, and SEE preparation."*

## 2. Updates to the About Us Page (`about.html`)

The **"Our Journey"** timeline section was completely rewritten using the "Journey" segment of the provided document to accurately reflect the school's history.

**Changes Made:**
*   **2011:** Added the foundation of the biodynamic farm on 8 acres.
*   **2014:** Added the opening of the Waldorf Kindergarten with 15 children.
*   **2015:** Added the beginning of Grade 1.
*   **2022:** Added the completion of the first Class Teacher cycle.
*   **2023:** Added the upgrade to High School (Class 9 and 10).
*   **2025:** Added the milestone of the first three students passing the SEE board examination.

## 3. Updates to Parent Education Page (`parent-education.html`)

The **"Grade-Specific Parenting Sessions"** section was restructured to match the three primary developmental stages from the homepage ("Classes Available" section). It was also enhanced with philosophical snippets extracted from the curriculum document.

**Changes Made:**
*   **Kindergarten Parents:** Integrated philosophy on why it's free of academics: *"Learn why Waldorf kindergarten is intentionally free of academic instruction, giving children time to be fully, joyfully young."*
*   **Grade 1-8 Parents:** Consolidated Grades 1-3 and 4-6 to match the main program structure. Focused on the class teacher journey and the expanding worldview: *"Understand the class teacher journey—where one dedicated teacher stays with the class—and support your child as their horizon expands outward from self to community."*
*   **Class 9-10 Parents:** Updated the title to "Class 9-10" and highlighted the balance of rigor and art for older students: *"Learn how we honor this profound transformation, providing intellectual rigor and SEE preparation without losing artistic wholeness and inner resilience."*
