Parent Education

A collaborative journey of meaningful relationships, Steiner-Waldorf pedagogy, and shared community.

The Shared Journey

Education at Maitreya Pathshala is a deep partnership. We walk this path together with families, ensuring every child is seen, heard, and supported across both home and school. Our relationship with parents is the foundation of our students' growth.

Parent Education & Community

Raising children to become happy, healthy, thriving adults is a shared odyssey between families and school.

Maitreya Pathshala is designed to engage not only the students but their entire families. We believe in meaningful relationships, where our trained teachers work with families over multiple years, understanding how to best support each individual child. From home visits to one-to-one parent sessions and regular conferences, we connect school life and home life to support the whole family.

Parent Education Series

Deepening our understanding of Steiner-Waldorf pedagogy and child development through regular sessions and dialogue.

Core Philosophy

Why Waldorf "waits" on academics and how teaching the "Head, Heart, and Hands" builds resiliency and flexibility in students.

Simplicity Parenting

Creating a peaceful home environment through healthy rhythms and our school's perspective on media and technology.

Developmental Stages

Grade-specific sessions focusing on milestones from early childhood through the transformative high school years.

A Connected Community

Events like our Spring Fair and home-school conferences are where our community truly comes to life.

At Maitreya Pathshala, we believe in a unified support system for every child. Our parents contribute through guest talks, artistic workshops (like woodwork and seasonal crafts), and project sessions that enrich classroom learning.

We prioritize meaningful relationships through regular home visits and one-to-one parent conferences. By celebrating the rhythm of the seasons together—from house building to festival celebrations—we model purposeful community for our children.

Our Shared Commitment

Education is a collaborative journey between families, teachers, and school. We ask for a conscious commitment to support your child's growth and the school community.

Home Rhythms

  • Sleep: At least 10 hours for proper rest.
  • Nutrition: A healthy breakfast to start the day.
  • Media: Limiting digital exposure for wonder.
  • Boundaries: Setting age-appropriate limits.

Active Participation

  • Events: Attending meetings and festivals.
  • Presence: On-time arrival and proper dress.
  • Service: Volunteering for school activities.
  • Newsletter: Reading school correspondence.

Communication

  • Directness: Open dialogue with teachers.
  • Respect: Protecting childhood from adult topics.
  • Growth: Sharing feedback for development.
  • Resolution: Collaborative problem solving.

Teacher Excellence

Our educators are dedicated mentors who evolve alongside the children.

Continuous Growth & Pedagogy
  • Holistic Training: Deep focus on child psychology and Waldorf principles.
  • International Mentorship: Workshops with global experts like Michal Ben Shalom.
  • Local Insight: Aligning Waldorf traditions with Nepal's National Education Policy.
  • Inner Growth: Weekly child study and sessions with Ritman Gurung (Eurythmy Therapy).
